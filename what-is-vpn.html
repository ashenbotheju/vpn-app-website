How Does a VPN Work?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a tool you can use to encrypt your internet traffic. When you browse the web without a VPN, your data can be tracked and recorded by your ISP (internet service provider) and the websites you visit. Your data may also be vulnerable to snoops and cybercriminals.

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) connection encrypts your traffic, hiding online activity from ISPs, and changes your IP to the server's location.

What Does a VPN Do?

Mask Your IP Address

Your IP (Internet Protocol) address, assigned by your ISP, gives the websites you visit your approximate location. A VPN gives you a new virtual location when you connect to one of its servers. This changes your IP to make it seem like you’re connected to the internet from the server's location.

Unblock Content on Any Network

While traveling abroad, you may find you can't access the same content you usually do when you’re at home. Your school or work may even block you from accessing certain websites and platforms. A VPN lets you access all your favorite content on any network.

Encrypt Your Internet Traffic

When you use a VPN to encrypt your internet connection, you keep prying eyes away from your traffic. That includes snoops, your ISP, and even the government. Public Wi-Fi may be convenient, but it’s also a hotbed for cybercrime. A VPN will protect you when you connect to public networks.

Protect All Your Devices

VPNs aren't just for computers. You can install VPN apps on your mobile devices, your Smart TV, and even your router. Securing all your devices with a VPN will encrypt all incoming and outgoing traffic and protect your digital life.

Why Should YouUse a VPN?

Protect All Your Devices

Free public Wi-Fi is handy when you’re on the go. Sadly, it is unsafe and makes you vulnerable even to basic cyberattacks. A VPN encrypts your online data and secures your personal information.

Mask your browsing

Masking your IP address is essential to secure online privacy. A Virtual Private Network ensures that your location and browsing history are hidden. This way, you can surf without anyone trying to steer your choices.

Unlock censored websites

Access to various websites is restricted in many countries due to geoblocking and censorship. You can unblock sites by connecting to a VPN server based in a different country.
